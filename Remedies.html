ALOE VERA

How do you apply it to the hair?

1. cut a leaf from a live aloe vera plant.
2. using a spoon, collect the gel (some like to blend it with coconut or olive oil)
3. rub the gel or mixture directly into the scalp and let it sit for an hour.
4. use a mild shampoo to wash the hair and scalp.
5. repeat this process 2-3 times a week, as needed.

OLIVE OIL

How do you apply it to the hair?

1. Simply mix 2 tablespoons of olive oil with 4-5 drops of tea tree oil.
2. Comb the mixture through your hair, starting at the scalp and working your way down to the ends of the hair.
3.Allow the mask to sit for 30 minutes and then rinse out.

GINGER

How do you apply it to the hair?

1. As a nourishing hair mask that targets hair loss and dryness.
2.Put a few pieces of aloe vera and some ginger juice into a blender, and blend until smooth.
3.Apply this mixture to your roots and lengths for an hour before rinsing it off

ONION

How do you apply it to the hair?

1. Apply the onion juice directly to your scalp and hair roots.
2.Massage your scalp for a few minutes to ensure that the onion juice is evenly distributed.
3.Leave the onion juice on your hair for at least 30 minutes to an hour.
4.Rinse your hair thoroughly with a mild shampoo and cool water.

BANANA HAIR MASK

How do you apply it to the hair?

1. Mash one ripe banana until it forms a smooth paste.
2.You can add a tablespoon of honey or yoghurt for extra moisture and shine if desired.
3.Apply the mixture to your hair, covering all strands from roots to tips.
4.Leave it on for 20-30 minutes.
5.Rinse thoroughly with shampoo and water.
